# 🚀 BezPlanner  
One-stop web app that turns raw startup ideas into investor-ready business plans and guides founders through every required legal document.

## Overview

Starting up is hard. Between market research, financials, and legal paperwork, founders spend weeks cobbling together a plan instead of building product. **BePlanner** does the heavy lifting: answer a guided questionnaire, and the platform auto-generates a complete business plan plus a checklist of required legal documents and where to obtain them.

**Target users**  
- Pre-se and early-stage founders  
- Accelerators & incubators  
- No-code startup operators

**Key idea**  
🧠 **LLM + web scraping + ✅ legal checklist** = **investor-ready plan + compliance roadmap** in minutes.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🧩 **Guided questionnaire** — step-by step walkthrough
- 📊 **Automated market sizing & competitive research** (live web scraping)
- 💰 **Financial projections** (revenue, burn, runway)
- 📄 **Legal document checklist** (by stage: incorporation, SAFE, IP assignment, etc.)
- 📎 **Sources & citations** (all scraped data is footnoted)
- 🌓 **Light/Dark theme** (Tailwind + prefers-color-scheme)
- 📱 **PWA-ready** (service-worker + offline fallback)
- 🛠️ **RESTful API** — head friendly (no session pinning)
